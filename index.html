FIVERR: Fiverr offers similar job opportunities as most general freelance job web sites; however, it works differently from rest of them. The authenticity of this platform and payment system is its’ main selling point. As a freelance registered on Fiverr, you have to create “gigs” based on your skillsets/expertise. In brief, you tell the client what you will do for them, budget for the job, time frame to complete their job then the clients decide whether they are interested in paying for your services. Fiverr Projects are priced minimum $5 (hence the name).
